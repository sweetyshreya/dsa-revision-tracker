Spaced-repetition revision for coding problems

📚 How It Works

After completing a revision, click ✓ Finished Revision.

The tracker schedules your next revision using spaced repetition:

1 day 3 days 7 days 14 days 30 days

After the 5th revision, the problem enters Monthly Revision Mode. Every future revision is scheduled after 30 days to keep the concept fresh without making the gap too long.
